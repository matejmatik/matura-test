Reversal Chart Patterns

A reversal pattern is simply a change in the prevailing direction of a stock's price trend. ...
The price highs and lows following the reversal would be lower than the highs and lows before it.
A reversal pattern can also occur at the end of a downtrend,
if the stock price begins steadily rising and produces higher highs.
